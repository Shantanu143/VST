About Us

VST Buildcon Private Limited, a premier construction company, offers luxurious apartments, upscale shops, and top-notch offices. It is led by three partners, each with a unique influence.

The "V" honors Vaishali Samant, a renowned Marathi singer and the company's brand ambassador and partner, adding musical allure to our identity.

The "S" pays homage to Shree Swami Samarth, symbolizing our commitment to values and spirituality. The "T" represents Thakur Builders, led by Purushottam Raghunathsingh Thakur, ensuring expertise and reliability in our projects.

Together, these pillars define VST Buildcon, where innovation and tradition converge to create exceptional spaces for our clients.

Vaishali Samant

Vaishali Samant, our esteemed partner, is more than just a celebrated singer; she embodies the very essence of excellence, dedication, and artistic passion that defines VST Buildcon. Her association with our company is a testament to our commitment to delivering nothing short of perfection. Just as her melodious voice resonates with heartfelt emotions, her presence enriches our mission to create exceptional living spaces.

Vaishali's unwavering commitment to her craft and versatility mirror the versatility in our construction projects, where every detail is meticulously curated for a harmonious experience. She brings a unique dimension to VST Buildcon, emphasizing our unwavering pursuit of quality and the creation of homes that echo her timeless music—infused with passion and perfection.
